about us

Nestled amidst the serene majesty of the mountains, Ehden Country Club invites you to experience a haven of relaxation and adventure. As a distinguished hotel and resort, we offer a sanctuary where guests can embrace the enchantment of every season. In the winter months, our rustic fireside warmth beckons weary travelers to unwind in cozy comfort, while savoring the tranquil beauty of snow-covered landscapes. Meanwhile, summertime unveils a playground of vitality, where guests can rejuvenate in our refreshing pools, engage in spirited tennis matches, and sculpt their bodies in our state-of-the-art gym facilities.

At Ehden Country Club, Wellness And Indulgence Harmonize Seamlessly, As Our Restaurant Tantalizes Taste Buds With A Fusion Of Healthy And Delectable Cuisine Crafted From Locally-Sourced Ingredients. Whether Seeking Solace Or Adventure, Our Retreat Promises An Unforgettable Escape Where Every Moment Is Infused With Unparalleled Tranquility And Vitality.
